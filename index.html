  • BBOXX CRM Application
    (Internal Application, no links available)

    The BBOXX CRM is the largest project I've worked at BBOXX (around a full year of work). The application is an hybrid (desktop and web) application working with node-webkit.
    This application is currently used in 20 shops in Kenya and Rwanda and has been developed with unique challenges in terms of connectivity.
